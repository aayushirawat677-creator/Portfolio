AAYUSHI · HUMAN_IN_THE_LOOP

I build the agents people actually keep talking to.

I'm Aayushi — an AI Product Manager with 3+ years taking LLM-driven agents from blank page to live product. Most recently I co-founded and led product for Lumi, a closed-loop behavioral AI agent that reasons over behavioral data and intervenes in real time across voice and text. It reached 83% retention and a 90 NPS, with validated willingness to pay at $40/month.

My edge is that I think in systems, not screens. I can spec an agent's reasoning, memory and tool use; define what "good" actually means with real AI-quality metrics — accuracy, tone, helpfulness — and translate fuzzy user needs into the architecture, evals and guardrails engineers can ship. I move fast: Wizard-of-Oz tests and ~2.5 experiments a week, fluent in the modern agent stack, so I hold my own in any room full of engineers.

What sets me apart: I pair IIT-Kharagpur systems rigor with a Harvard management lens and an operator's bias for shipping — across health, voice AI, commerce and telemedicine, in three countries.

Off the clock, I'm a relentless home cook — MISO, my recipe-optimization agent, literally started as me trying to solve my own "what do I cook tonight?" problem. I also run long, get lost in decision-science books, and collect a few too many half-finished side projects.
